Reducing Our Carbon Footprint

Well-Sourced Produce

Make sure to buy produce form local farms and vendors so that you aren't contributing to pollution from cargo planes.

Certified Businesses

Try to make use of businesses listed on our website that are sustainable and environmentally friendly.

A carbon footprint represents the amount of greenhouse gas emissions generated by human activities. It is important to reduce our carbon footprint because excessive emissions contribute to climate change, which leads to severe environmental consequences. By reducing our carbon footprint, we can mitigate the harmful effects of climate change, preserve natural resources, and improve air quality. This can be achieved through energy conservation, adopting renewable energy sources, sustainable transportation choices, and embracing eco-friendly practices.

Modern Farming

Modern farming reduces carbon emmissions by only shipping locally without the use of planes which burn a lot of fuel.

Farmer Communication

Communicate with local farmers about new produce and the location they will sell at.
